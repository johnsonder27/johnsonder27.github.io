Logitech G430

Today I will be reviewing the Logitech G430 surround sound gaming headset.


I rated the Logitech G430 on 4 things comfort, sound quality, mic quality, and durability.

  1. Comfort, the headset is very comfortable with their ear and head cushions. The ear cushions completely surround your ear blocking outside noise and for a more immersive gaming experience. The head cushion is very stiff but over time it will get softer.
  2. Sound quality, the sound quality is pretty good for gaming and tasks like watching YouTube but the headset does not have a ton of bass. In gaming you can tell where enemy foot steps are pretty well. It does have sound control on the cable along with a mic mute switch as well.
  3. Mic quality, the mic quality is average. It is good for Skype calls and gaming but that's about it.
  4. Durability, the chord is very durable and I haven’t had a probably yet. Some reviews on amazon have said the plastic parts have broke but I have had mine for 3 months and I’ve had no problems. If you don't trash the headset it should last a very long time.


In conclusion I would recommend the Logitech G430. It is a great budget gaming headset.
